# HIRA

Your daily companion for hijrah, reflection, and impact.

## 🌙 What is HIRA?

HIRA is an Islamic super app designed to help Muslims:
- Stay consistent in their prayers
- Read and understand the Qur’an
- Track their spiritual journey
- Contribute to meaningful causes

## 🚀 Our Mission

To empower the next generation of Muslims with a modern, accessible, and impactful digital ecosystem.

## 🛠️ Core Features

- 📖 Qur’an (multi-language, tafsir, audio)
- 🕌 Prayer times & Qibla
- 📿 Dzikir & daily duas
- 🤝 Crowdfunding & zakat
- 📊 Hijrah progress tracker

## 🤝 Contributing

We welcome contributors who want to build impactful Islamic technology.

## 🌍 Vision

To become the leading Islamic super app globally.
